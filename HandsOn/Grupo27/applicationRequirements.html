Application: "Air quality visualizer"

1. The application will offer relevant information about the air quality of the province of Madrid and its municipalities to the user.

2. The user will be able to filter the offered information about the air quality using parameters such as:

  • Date: (Year, month, day and also combinations of these parameters)
  • Municipality.
  • Hour interval (HH-MM).
  • A series of predefined magnitudes: (such as Nitrogen oxides, metaxylene, etc.).
